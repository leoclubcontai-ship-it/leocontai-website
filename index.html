About Us

Where there's a need,
there's a Leo

"Leadership, Experience, Opportunity" — that's what makes a Leo. Members embody the best qualities of our incredible organization.

Leo Club of Contai was founded on 14 April 1980 as the youth wing of Lions Club of Contai. As an Omega Leo Club (ages 18–30), we provide young adults the chance to develop leadership, teamwork, and compassion through meaningful community service.

Together with Lions, we continue our mission to create young leaders who serve with heart, courage and responsibility across health, education, environment and humanitarian causes.
